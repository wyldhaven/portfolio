Wyldhaven

Wyldhaven is a music producer based out of Madison, Wisconsin. The concept for the Wyldhaven website was to be a simple but stricking announcement for his new album. The goal was to quickly direct users to where they can hear the album and to increase visibility to his social media accounts. We were looking for an unforgettable site and added to the brand of Wyldhaven.

Works in Progress

Over the course of designing this site, I went through a few iterations before I decided that photography-based design would be best the route towards being unforgettable. While I loved the idea of a single-page scrolling experience to keep the user on track, I just felt that it needed something more. I encourged and assisted with the new photo for the website which I feel not only gave me the inspiration to complete my design, but truly added to the brand of the artist.
